Getting Started

  • Sign up

    Enter your basic information + phone number

  • Complete Profile

    Answer a few questions to help us get to know you and your vehicle better!

  • Vehicle Inspection

    Don’t forget to bring:
    Phone with Lyft app
    Driver’s license
    Car + Car insurance

  • Drive

    You could be ready to drive in as little as a day!

Hit the road!

Once you’ve gone through the steps above:

  1. Open the app
  2. Tap ‘Get Started’
  3. Enter your account’s phone number
  4. You’re ready for your first rider!

Using the App

Tap the ‘go online’ button.
Once you’re online, you’re able to receive ride requests.

Once you receive a request, you’ll see a notification.
Tap anywhere to accept.

Tap the top-right arrow next to the pickup location to begin navigation to your rider’s address.

Tap ‘Confirm arrival,’ and we’ll send a text to the rider to let them know you’ve arrived.

When the passenger gets in your car and you’ve confirmed it’s the right person, tap ‘Pick up Stephanie’.

Follow the route to the rider’s destination and tap ‘Tap to drop off’ when you arrive.

Tap ‘Confirm drop off’ to end the ride.

Rate the passenger then leave feedback.
Passengers won’t see this feedback, only Lyft support.
