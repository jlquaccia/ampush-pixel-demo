5 Reasons Driving for Lyft is Better than Getting a Job

  • 1. No Experience Needed

    Let’s face it, finding a new job can be a grind. Filling out applications, perfecting your resume, and trying to impress in your interview takes a lot of time and energy, and getting rejected can be downright discouraging. With Lyft, all you need is a car and some free time, and you’re ready to go.

  • 2. Be Your Own Boss, and Make Your Own Hours

    Good bosses are few and far between. But as a Lyft driver, you make the rules. Drive when you want, on the schedule that fits your life—not someone else’s.

  • 3. New Driver Bonus

    Right now, Lyft is offering a $500 Bonus to new drivers who complete 150 rides in their first 45 days. That’s on top of your regular ride earnings. What’s better than free cash?

  • 4. Meet New People and Explore Your City

    Driving for Lyft isn’t just about the cash and flexibility, it can also be a pretty fun gig! Riders are often talkative and appreciate their friendly drivers. A few good stories and jokes can go a long way to racking up the tips! As you get to know the city, you can share your recommendations for the best restaurants, bars, and attractions in the area.

  • 5. Time for Your Passion Projects

    Many would-be entrepreneurs just can’t bring themselves to leave the security of steady income to follow their dreams and build their own business. As a Lyft driver, you don’t have to choose between your passion and a solid income. You can make time for what matters, and open the app and start earning whenever you want. With Lyft, driving on your own time means living on your own terms.
