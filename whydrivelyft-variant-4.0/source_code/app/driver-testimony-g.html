Mark S.

Miami, FL
2014 Hyundai Elantra

"You’re essentially running your own business, and how you run it can make a huge difference. It’s up to you to make sure your customers have a great experience."

  • What made you decide to drive for Lyft?

    I signed up with Lyft because I needed extra income to help pay the bills, plus I wanted to see what all the hype was about.

  • How was the signup process?

    The signup process didn’t take long at all, probably about a week and half from sign up to being approved to drive. I was informed as to what step they were at, and also informed of every step approval. I had to drive one hour to get my vehicle inspection done for free from Lyft. Perhaps that can be streamlined a bit better.

  • What is your favorite part about driving for Lyft?

    My favorite part of driving for Lyft would be the fact that I can make my own hours and have no boss.

  • Are there any downsides?

    We pay for our own gas, our own maintenance, and don’t start getting paid until the passenger gets in. So at times you may drive 15 minutes to pick up a call that only goes 5 minutes away.

  • About how much time do you spend driving for Lyft each week?

    I spend approximately 30 hours per week driving for Lyft.

  • Do you have any advice or tips for new drivers?

    For new drivers, it is important that they understand that you’re essentially running your own business, and how you run it can make a huge difference. It’s up to you to make sure your customers have a great experience (which usually results in better tips and ratings!), but at the same time don’t let the passengers take advantage of you; they will ask for extra rides, extra distances etc...stick to the app and ask them to enter in the new destination if they want to change it.

  • Any final thoughts / comments?

    I have driven for Lyft in Fort Lauderdale, Miami, Palm Beach County, Denver, Glenwood Springs. While there are some downsides, Lyft gives me the flexibility to work on my own schedule, be my own boss, and work in many different places.
