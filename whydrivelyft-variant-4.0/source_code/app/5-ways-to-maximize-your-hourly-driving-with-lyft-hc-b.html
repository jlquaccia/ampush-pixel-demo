5 Ways to Make Serious Cash Driving with Lyft

Most rideshare drivers agree that simply driving more hours is not necessarily the best way to earn more. Lyft drivers can find plenty of opportunities to “work smarter, not harder”, and get the most out of the hours they are already driving. Here are the top 5 tips you need to take advantage of to put more money in your pocket as a Lyft driver.

  • 1. Drive During Peak Hours and Prime Time

    Just like other ridesharing services, Lyft has peak hours when demand is highest, and drivers can capitalize. You can usually count on rush hour in the mornings and evenings as people are commuting to and from work. Pro tip: pay attention to the weather forecast. More people will opt for a Lyft on a rainy day to make sure they stay dry on the way to their destination.

  • 2. Earn Tips (and Keep 100% of Them)

    With Lyft, tips you earn are yours and yours only—every penny tipped goes directly to the driver. Lyft has also done a great job of designing a system in which tips are encouraged and appreciated. For example, it allows passengers to tip their drivers through the app, a feature that Uber resisted including in its application for years. Go the extra mile (no pun intended) to provide your riders with a comfortable, clean, and safe ride, and watch your tips (and your driver rating) rise.

  • 3. Get Big Bonuses for Referrals

    Referral bonuses are not just limited to Lyft passengers. The company loves to reward its drivers with referral bonuses as well. You can log into your application and then the referral portal to receive your unique driver’s referral code. Lyft allows you to share your referral code on any platform you want, so take advantage of your instant messaging apps, social networks, or your personal website.

  • 4. Take Advantage of Bonus Offers

    Lyft offers bonuses and incentives in different regions at different times to give drivers an opportunity to earn more. In most cases, you will be required to drive more, take more rides and get better ratings from your customers to qualify for the bonus rewards. Power Driver by Lyft is one such incentive that you can bank on. Lyft often makes power driver bonuses available with a tiered reward model, so as you drive more, your bonus increases.

  • 5. Take Advantage of Guarantees

    In addition to bonuses, Lyft allows drivers to opt into average hourly or weekly guarantees. This means that eligible drivers who opt in will be paid the guaranteed rate even if their actual earnings are lower during the guaranteed time period. So, if you’ve given a couple of rides during guaranteed hours, but have made less than the guaranteed hourly rate, Lyft will pay you for the difference! Guarantees require drivers to meet certain criteria, and availability varies based on time and location.
