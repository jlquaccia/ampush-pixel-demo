Efrain S.

New York, NY
2012 Toyota Corolla

"It's a great way to make some extra cash! I’m a student, and also do other freelance work, so Lyft allows me to work on a flexible schedule."

  • What made you decide to drive for Lyft?

    Friends of mine had driven and told me it was a great way to make some extra cash! I’m a student, and also do other freelance work, so Lyft allows me to work on a flexible schedule.

  • What are your favorite parts of driving for Lyft?

    The ease and flexible schedule, and I love meeting and talking to new people all day long!

  • Are there any downsides?

    As with anything, there are downsides. Long days, heavy traffic, and lots of sitting can be difficult. The occasional navigation failures can also cause issues.

  • Do you have favorite times of day to drive?

    I prefer mornings and daytime over evenings. People are usually starting their morning and are less stressed and cranky. There is sunlight out and a positive start to the day.

  • What tips or advice would you give to new drivers?

    Remember: All it takes is ONE negative review or ONE positive one to affect your schedule and the rest of your day. Make it count. Go above and beyond for people so that they tip you and rate you not just GOOD, but as OUTSTANDING! Make people smile. Ask them about their day. Care!

  • Any final thoughts/comments?

    Lyft is a great side gig that can turn into a full-time, lifetime career with relative ease, for those “special few”. It takes a special person to be a Lyft driver, and a great one, at that. I like to think that, in my short time, I am “getting there” but have a few steps to go still! Lyft and similar services are the future!!!!! (:
