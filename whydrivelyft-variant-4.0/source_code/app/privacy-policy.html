Privacy Policy

Information Collection

For users who submit information in the driver application form, we collect the applicant’s name, e-mail address, phone number, and city. This information is submitted to Lyft to create your driver profile. This information is not shared with other organizations for commercial purposes without user consent. Non-applicants may use the portions of the WhyDriveLyft.com site that do not require application. The IP addresses of these users are stored.

In addition to personal information, we may also collect information that is in no way personally identifiable. This may include your operating system, your internet browser, the domain name of your Internet service provider, and the web sites you visit directly before and after your visit to WhyDriveLyft.com.

How does WhyDriveLyft.com use my collected information?

Personal Driver Application information is submitted directly to Lyft to create your Driver Profile and begin the application process, and is not shared with other organizations for commercial purposes. The information you provide us may also be used by Lyft or WhyDriveLyft.com to contact you via email, text message, or other means to provide support, reminders, and other information. Only WhyDriveLyft.com and Lyft will have access to your personally identifiable information, and you will never be contacted by any party other than WhyDriveLyft.com, Lyft, or Lyft partners. The only exceptions to the foregoing are that we may disclose personally identifiable information (i) in response to legal process, for example, in response to a court order or subpoena, (ii) in response to a law enforcement agency’s request, or where we believe it is necessary to investigate, prevent, or take actions regarding illegal activities, suspected fraud, situations involving potential threats to the physical safety of another person, violations of our terms of use, or as otherwise required by law, or (iii) in the event we are acquired by or merged with another company.

Cookies

WhyDriveLyft.com uses cookies to store visitors preferences, record session information, record user-specific information on what pages users access or visit, alert visitors to new areas that we think might be of interest to them when they return to our site, customize web page content based on visitors’ browser type or other information that the visitor sends.

User Comments

Driver testimonials, blog comments or any other information submitted through our website become the sole property of WhyDriveLyft.com. We reserve the right to delete or remove reviews, comments or other user submitted information for any reason. However, we will never edit user submitted reviews or comments for being too critical or too complimentary.

Security

We have put in place appropriate physical, electronic and managerial procedures to safeguard and help prevent unauthorized access, maintain data security and correctly use the information we collect.

Changes to the Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy can change at any time and changes are effective upon posting. Please check back frequently for updates as it is your sole responsibility to be aware of changes. We do not provide notices of changes in any manner other than by posting the changes at this Site page.
