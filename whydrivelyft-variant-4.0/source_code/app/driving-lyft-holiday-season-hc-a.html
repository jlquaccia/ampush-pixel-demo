How Driving for Lyft Saved My Holiday Season
(And My Bank Account)

  • It’s that time of year again! Time to make travel plans, pick out great gifts for friends and family, seek out the holiday deals, and then pray your bank account makes it through to January without any irreversible damage.

  • I usually pride myself on keeping a tight budget, and having the discipline to add a little to my savings each month. But without fail, every year the holiday season ends up cutting into my budget and pushing my savings into the red. That is, until last year, when a friend turned me onto a great new way to make extra cash during the holiday season — Driving for Lyft!

  • Lyft has been an absolute life-saver for my December budget, and it’s an especially great gig this time of year for a few reasons.

    1. It’s flexible. I can work as much or as little as I want to, and can easily fit in my driving time outside my normal work schedule.

Tons of people are traveling to see family and friends, and they need to get around. New Year’s Eve is one of the biggest days of the year for drivers, as people flock to parties, bars, and events and rely on their Lyft driver to get them home safe.

  • Driving for Lyft has completely changed my holiday season. No more pinching pennies, no more dipping into my savings, and no more mini heart attacks when I look at my bank statement! Lyft has made it easy to keep my budget on track, and afford a great holiday season.
